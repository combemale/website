Journals

  • Deputy Editor in Chief of the platinum opeen access journal JOT on Software and language engineering.
  • Member of the Editorial Boards of the Springer Journal on Software and Systems Modeling (SoSyM), the Springer Software Quality Journal (SQJ), the Elsevier Journal of Computer Languages (COLA), and the Elsevier Journal on Science of Computer Programming (SCP, Advisory Board of the Software Section).
