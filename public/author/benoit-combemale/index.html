I am Full Professor of Software Engineering at the University of Rennes 1. I am Head of the Computer Science Department at the engineering school ESIR, and researcher within the research team DiverSE, joint to the CNRS Research Institute of Computer Science and Random Systems (IRISA) and Inria. I am also adjunct researcher in the SM@RT team of the CNRS Research Institute in Computer Science of Toulouse (IRIT). More information in my complete bio.
